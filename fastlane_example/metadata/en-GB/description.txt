Typpo is a typo checker and forensic typography companion for writers, editors, designers, marketers, developers, students, and anyone who cares how text looks and reads.

Paste or type any text and Typpo finds the issues spellcheck usually ignores: straight quotes, fake ellipses, wrong dash usage, double spaces, inconsistent sentence spacing, widows, orphans, prime-mark mistakes, multiplication-sign mistakes, trademark symbol fallbacks, and even Comic Sans in rich text.

WHY PEOPLE USE TYPPO

• Catch hidden mistakes before you publish, send, or share
• Get a crime score and clear verdict for every text sample
• Review every issue with context, explanation, and suggested fix
• Apply supported fixes, then copy the cleaned text
• Share polished report cards as images, stories, landscape cards, text, or links
• Keep a searchable history of past case files
• Open the Law Library to learn why each issue matters and how to fix it
• Tune detection rules, scoring strictness, dash style, theme, haptics, sounds, and language

BUILT FOR REAL TEXT WORK

Typpo is useful for social posts, product copy, emails, articles, captions, landing pages, specs, UI strings, brand guidelines, student work, and final proofreading passes. If a paragraph feels almost right but not quite polished, Typpo helps you find out why.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

All analysis runs on your device. Your reports are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. No account setup, no copy-paste to a web app, and no need to hand your text to another editor just to spot the small things.

INCLUDED IN THE APP

• Forensic scan flow with live progress
• Detailed issue breakdown with severity and context
• Clean-text copy action after supported fixes
• Report sharing and export tools
• Saved case history with bulk delete and export
• Built-in multilingual UI with 30 supported languages

Typpo is not a grammar course or a generic spellchecker. It is a focused typography and proofreading companion built to catch the details that make polished text feel truly finished.
